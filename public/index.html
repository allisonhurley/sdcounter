COVID-19 Proofing

A Senior CE Design Project

MISSION STATEMENT

To provide a platform aiding social distancing

Our mission is to create a proof of concept for a system capable of tracking social distancing in various locations. The product would be used by businesses to track the current number of people in a building, as well as to log occupancy trends over time. Users can navigate the website to view this data and plan their day around social distancing.
