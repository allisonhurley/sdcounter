MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission statement

Creating a proof of concept of a system capable of tracking and improving the aspect of social distancing for users. The product would be used by locations to track the current number of people in the building and show trends over time. Users would be able to view these live numbers as well as use trends to better plan their day and trips.
